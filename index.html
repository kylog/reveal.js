Alert fatigue

Two talks referenced an NIH study of alarm fatigue in medical settings

72-99% of alarms deemed insignificant

"Important clinical events may be missed amid the cacophony"

Alarm fatigue not just for squeaky wheel alarms

Alert fatigue - approaches

Only alert on customer facing conditions

Metrics on non-customer facing conditions are for observability, not alerting

Schedule regular (quarterly) review of alerts

Retire low ROI alerts

Fewer alert sources takes work and discipline
